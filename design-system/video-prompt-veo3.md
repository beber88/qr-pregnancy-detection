# Veo3 Prompt — Demo Video: How to Photograph

## Primary Prompt (English — Veo3 works best in English)

```
A clean, soft, instructional video showing how to photograph a urine sample for a health app. Warm, feminine aesthetic with cream and terracotta tones. No text overlays. No faces shown.

Scene 1 (0-5s): A woman's hand gently places a small transparent glass cup containing pale yellow liquid onto a white sheet of paper on a light wooden table. Soft natural daylight from a window. The hand withdraws smoothly.

Scene 2 (5-10s): A smartphone is held about 15-20cm directly above the cup, camera pointing straight down. We see the phone screen showing the cup from above. The hands are steady. The angle is perfectly perpendicular to the table.

Scene 3 (10-15s): A finger taps the screen's capture button. A subtle, soft shutter animation. The phone screen shows the captured image of the cup from above. The hands lower the phone gently.

Style: Minimal, warm, calming. Similar to a premium skincare brand tutorial. Soft focus background. No clinical or medical feel. Natural light only, no flash. Color palette: warm whites, cream, soft terracotta accents. Shot on iPhone aesthetic. 
Aspect ratio: 9:16 (vertical/portrait).
Duration: 15 seconds.
No audio. No text. No logos.
```
